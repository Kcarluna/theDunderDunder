About Me

Hope you enjoyed DunderDunder!

As you may have noticed... this isn't your typical portfolio. Hence, the name. DunderDunder is a step above a portfolio: a "living" portofolio. An ever evolving display of technique, creativiity, and mastery. I challenge you, as the viewer, to follow me along this journey and analyze some of the overarching themes that I'm exploring. By no means am I a professional, so I encourage you to provide feedback (any format) that'll help me along my travels.

Who am I? I'm Jacob! An amateur engineer with no formal experience in this field, but passionate enough to force my way in!! Or at least try. Starting as a complete amateur and recording my trials and tribulations will not only benefit me, but you too!! Whether you're in this field or not. If I leave you with anything it's this: Follow your Dreams!! Leave the self-doubt behind and listen to that voice in your head and achieve greatness.

Check back again for constant updates!
